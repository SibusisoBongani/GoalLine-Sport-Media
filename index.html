By Azola Ngxanga | 3 June 2025

SuperSport United has been sold to Siwelele Football Club (Pty) Ltd in a deal valued at around R50 million, pending approval from the Premier Soccer League (PSL). This marks the end of an era for the club, which has been a PSL mainstay since its founding in 1994.
